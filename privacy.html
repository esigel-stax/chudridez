Privacy Policy — Chudridez

Last updated: 23 July 2026

Chudridez is a free tool for planning Bay Area bicycle rides paired with food and drink stops. We aim to collect as little as possible. In short: Chudridez does not collect, store, or share any personal information on a server. There are no accounts and no ads.

Information we do NOT collect

We do not ask for your name, email, or login. We do not run analytics or advertising trackers, and we do not sell or share any data. Chudridez has no backend server of its own.

Information stored on your device

To make the app useful, some information is saved locally in your browser or app storage (using localStorage) and never leaves your device:

  • Your bucket list of saved rides and food stops.
  • Any rides (GPX) or food stops you add yourself.
  • Cached map routes and preferences, so the app loads faster and works offline.
  • An optional Mapbox access token, if you choose to enter one, stored only in your browser.

You can clear all of this at any time using your browser/app settings, or the app's “Clear mine” control.

Location

If you use turn-by-turn or “Follow GPS” navigation, the app reads your device location to show your position on the map and give directions. This happens on your device and is not sent to us or stored. Your browser or phone will ask permission first, and you can decline.

Third-party services

Chudridez displays maps and routes using free, third-party services. When the app loads or routes a ride, standard web requests (which may include your IP address and the map area or coordinates being requested) are sent to these providers, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Map tiles: CARTO, Esri, and AWS terrain tiles.
  • Route calculation: the public OSRM router (and, only if you add a token, the Mapbox Directions API).
  • Elevation data: Open-Meteo.
  • Restaurant data: the San Francisco Chronicle’s public restaurant map feed.

We do not control these services and receive no personal data back from them.

Children

Chudridez is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
